Grade-by-Grade Curriculum

Four years. Four tracks. Complete coverage.

Each grade level builds on the last, creating a coherent 4-year pathway from AI awareness to AI fluency.

9th
AI Foundations

What is AI? How do AI systems learn?

Students explore the fundamentals of artificial intelligence, machine learning concepts, and the critical questions of bias and fairness in AI systems. Interactive simulations and real-world case studies ground abstract concepts in tangible experiences.

8 modules 12 hours
10th
AI & Data

Training data, privacy, surveillance & prompt engineering

Students dive into how AI models are trained, the role of data in shaping AI outcomes, privacy implications, and surveillance ethics. Introduction to prompt engineering gives students hands-on experience shaping AI outputs.

8 modules 14 hours
11th
AI & Your Career

AI impact by industry, AI-resilient careers & AI tools

Students analyze how AI is transforming every major industry, identify careers that are AI-resilient vs. AI-vulnerable, and learn to use professional AI tools. Career exploration is directly tied to readiness scores.

8 modules 14 hours
12th
AI in Practice

Advanced prompts, ethical AI, building with AI & entrepreneurship

Seniors master advanced prompt engineering, apply ethical frameworks to AI use in research and writing, build projects with AI tools, and explore AI-driven entrepreneurship. Capstone projects demonstrate applied AI fluency.

8 modules 16 hours
