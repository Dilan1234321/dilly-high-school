The Suite

Six tools. One platform.
Complete admissions intelligence.

Everything a student needs to find, apply to, and afford the right college — without a private consultant.

1

College Matcher

GPA + test scores + extracurriculars + essay quality feed a matching algorithm that generates a personalized Reach / Target / Safety list. Each school includes acceptance probability, tuition, net price, and key deadlines. The list updates dynamically as the student's profile improves.

2

Essay Grading Engine

Rubric-based scoring across six dimensions — authenticity, narrative structure, reflection, writing quality, distinctiveness, and fit. Includes AI detection, draft comparison, and voice protection.

Full essay grading details →
3

Application Timeline Manager

Every deadline tracked — Early Decision, Early Action, Regular Decision, financial aid, supplemental essays, interviews. Push notifications keep students on schedule. Counselors get full visibility into who is on track and who is falling behind.

4

Financial Aid Estimator

Expected Family Contribution (EFC) calculation, net price comparison across every school on the student's list, and a scholarship database with deadlines. Families see the real cost — not the sticker price — before they apply.

5

Recommendation Letter Manager

Helps students identify the best recommenders, generates personalized talking points for each teacher, and tracks request status. No more lost requests or last-minute scrambles.

6

Admissions Decision Tracker

Log admissions decisions as they arrive, compare financial aid packages side by side, and aggregate yield data at the school and district level. Turns individual outcomes into institutional intelligence.
