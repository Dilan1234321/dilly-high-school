Student Dashboard

Everything a student needs in one place.

The student dashboard is the command center for college and career readiness. Students see their scores, track progress, explore careers, complete AI coursework, and prepare for what comes after graduation—all from a single, intuitive interface.

🎓 Student View
  • Career Readiness Score — real-time Smart, Grit & Build metrics with progress tracking
  • Career Exploration — 22 career fields scored and ranked against their profile
  • AI Curriculum — 4-year course pathway with module progress and completion badges
  • College List — personalized college recommendations based on readiness alignment
  • Mock Interviews — AI-powered practice interviews with feedback and scoring
  • Academy Courses — supplemental skill-building courses beyond AI literacy
👪 Parent View
  • Score at a Glance — readiness score summary with trend indicators and dimension breakdown
  • Weekly Progress Emails — automated updates on coursework, score changes, and milestones
  • College Readiness Indicators — clear signals showing where their student stands vs. benchmarks
  • Career Field Explorer — see which careers match their student and why
  • Actionable Next Steps — specific recommendations parents can act on with their student
Parent Dashboard

Parents finally get visibility.

Parents have always been left in the dark when it comes to career readiness. Dilly changes that. The parent dashboard gives families a quantified, real-time view of where their student stands—and what to do next.

“No other platform gives parents a quantified, actionable career readiness view.”

Counselor Dashboard

Manage caseloads with precision.

School counselors are stretched thin. The counselor dashboard gives them the data-driven tools to prioritize interventions, track outcomes, and ensure no student falls through the cracks.

📋 Counselor View
  • Caseload Overview — all assigned students with readiness scores, trends, and status at a glance
  • At-Risk Alerts — automated flags for students showing declining scores or disengagement
  • Gap Analysis by Demographic — identify readiness disparities by race, gender, income level, and grade
  • Intervention Tracking — log interventions, track follow-ups, and measure impact over time
🏢 Administrator View
  • District-Wide Readiness Data — aggregate readiness scores across all schools, grades, and demographics
  • School Comparisons — benchmark schools against each other and identify top performers
  • Accreditation-Ready Reports — pre-built reports aligned to accreditation requirements
  • AI Literacy Compliance Tracking — monitor curriculum deployment and completion rates for state mandates
  • ROI Metrics — quantify platform impact: engagement rates, score improvements, career pathway starts
Administrator Dashboard

District-wide data, one click away.

Administrators need the big picture without drowning in spreadsheets. The admin dashboard delivers district-wide readiness analytics, compliance tracking, and accreditation-ready reporting in a single view.
