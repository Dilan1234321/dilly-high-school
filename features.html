The Readiness Architecture

Why One Score Is the Spine

The Career Readiness Score unifies every feature into a single, actionable metric. Three dimensions capture the full picture of student preparedness.

🧠

Smart

Academic mastery, AI literacy, critical thinking, and subject-area competency. Measured through coursework, assessments, and curriculum completion.

Cognitive Dimension
💪

Grit

Resilience, self-regulation, collaboration, and growth mindset. Captured through behavioral signals, engagement patterns, and self-assessments.

Character Dimension
🛠

Build

Applied skills, project completion, career exploration depth, and real-world readiness. Tracked through portfolios, mock interviews, and pathway progress.

Applied Dimension
Implementation

First Thirty Days

From contract signature to student-facing launch in four weeks. No heavy lift from IT.

Week 1
SSO & LMS Setup — Integrate with your district's identity provider (Clever, ClassLink, Google SSO) and connect to existing LMS (Canvas, Schoology, Google Classroom). Data import and rostering complete.
Week 2
Staff Training — Live training sessions for counselors, teachers, and administrators. Role-specific walkthroughs of dashboards, score interpretation, and curriculum tools.
Week 3
Student Onboarding — Guided first-login experience for students. Initial readiness assessment, career interest inventory, and personalized pathway setup.
Week 4
Monitoring & Parent Rollout — Activate parent dashboard access. Review early engagement metrics with your Dilly success manager. Ongoing support begins.
