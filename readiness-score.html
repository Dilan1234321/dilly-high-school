Three Dimensions

One score. Three dimensions.
The full picture.

Every student is more than a GPA. The Readiness Score captures knowledge, character, and capability in a single, trackable number.

S
Smart

Knowledge, analytical thinking, and academic preparation. The cognitive foundation every career requires.

Assessed via: GPA, course rigor, AI literacy scores, reading comprehension, quantitative reasoning.

94% of hiring managers prioritize critical thinking over degree prestige.
G
Grit

Resilience, leadership, and persistence. The qualities that separate those who succeed from those who quit.

Assessed via: Extracurriculars, work experience, mock interview performance, peer & teacher endorsements.

Strongest single predictor of long-term career success in longitudinal studies.
B
Build

Practical skills, portfolio quality, and industry certifications. Proof that a student can create, not just consume.

Assessed via: Course completions, certifications earned, portfolio artifacts, resume quality score.

92% of employers say graduates need more practical, applied skills.
